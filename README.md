# US-Food-Delivery-Market-Analysis
U.S. Food Delivery Market Competition Analysis

📌 Business Problem

The U.S. food delivery market is highly competitive. Restaurants must decide optimal pricing strategies while maintaining customer satisfaction. This project analyzes how pricing and quality affect demand using AI techniques.

⸻

🎯 Objectives
	•	Identify market segments using K-Means clustering
	•	Analyze price elasticity using Logistic Regression
	•	Provide revenue optimization insights

⸻

📊 Dataset

Uber Eats USA Restaurants & Menus
Source: Kaggle
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ahmedshahriarsakib/uber-eats-usa-restaurants-menus

⸻

🧠 Economic Concepts Applied
	•	Monopolistic Competition
	•	Price Elasticity of Demand
	•	Revenue Optimization
	•	Market Segmentation
	•	Risk Analysis

⸻

🤖 AI Techniques Used
	•	Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
	•	Exploratory Data Analysis
	•	K-Means Clustering
	•	Logistic Regression

⸻

📈 Key Findings
	1.	Most menu items are priced between $5–$20.
	2.	Market is heavily concentrated in mid-price segment.
	3.	Price has weak effect on demand probability.
	4.	Rating score strongly increases demand likelihood.
	5.	Market structure reflects monopolistic competition.
